The Approach
"This guide assumes you want to eat like someone who lives in these places, not like someone passing through. That means prioritizing the local and the seasonal over the famous, understanding why a dish matters before you order it, and knowing when to splurge and when to stand at a counter."

Japan does not have a cuisine. It has hundreds. Each destination in this guide covers the food culture, essential dishes, seasonal rhythms, drinking traditions, and dining etiquette of each location—the education you need before you sit down—followed by specific restaurant recommendations across every budget tier.
